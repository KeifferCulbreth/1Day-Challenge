    • Why I Chose These Two
      I originally was only going to pursue finance, because that is what I was most comfortable with and the major which members of my family had received. However, while I loved finance, I found computers to be fascinating. I was talking to one of my good friends about this and he recommended that I take just one programming class to see how I liked it. He thought it would match well with my love for problem solving and logical thinking. He was right and I fell in love with the topic. So, I figured the decision between these two areas did not need to be binary(pun intended) and that they would in fact make a powerful coupling that would make me a well rounded individual. This would allow me to contribute a unique skill set, an in depth knowledge and love for both finance and computer science.
